# GPA-Calculator
GPA &amp; Score Calculator designed specifically for our university's grading scale. It automatically converts percentages to grades, handles excluded/PE courses, saves your data locally so you never lose your progress, and even lets you compare your GPA with a friend! Just double-click the file to open it on your phone or laptop.
🎓 Ultimate University GPA & Score CalculatorWelcome to the Ultimate University GPA & Score Calculator! This is a smart, offline-capable, standalone web application designed specifically for our university's grading system. It calculates both your Weighted GPA and your Weighted Average Score (Percentage), taking the guesswork out of your academic progress.
🌟 Why This Calculator?Most online calculators use a generic 4.0 scale that doesn't match our specific university point system (where A* = 4.0, A- = 3.6, B+ = 3.3, etc.). This calculator was custom-built by analyzing actual university transcripts across multiple colleges (Business, Health Science Technology, Information Technology) to ensure 100% mathematical accuracy.
✨ Key Features
🪄 Magic Percentage Sync: Simply type your score (e.g., 86%), and the calculator will automatically snap to the correct letter grade (A-) and calculate the exact grade points.
💾 Auto-Save (Local Storage): Never lose your data! The app automatically saves your current semester courses and previous cumulative data directly to your browser. If you close the tab and come back tomorrow, everything will be exactly as you left it.
🛡️ Smart Exclusions (W, PE, E, FE): Not all courses affect your GPA. You can easily uncheck the "Include" box for Withdrawn, Pass/Exempt, or Excluded courses.
🤖 ACT Auto-Detection: The app automatically detects "ACT" courses (like Team Sports) and excludes them from the GPA calculation by default.
📊 Cumulative Tracking: Enter your previous GPA and Earned Credits (GPHRS) to instantly see how this semester affects your overall academic standing.
🏆 Friendly Comparison: Includes a hidden, collapsible tab that allows you to input a friend's GPA and Score to track who is currently in the lead!
📱 100% Mobile Responsive: Designed with Tailwind CSS to look beautiful and work perfectly on both desktop monitors and smartphone screens.
🚀 How to Use 
Add Your Courses: Click "Add Course" for each subject you are taking this semester.
Enter Grades: Type in your Credits (GPHRS) and your expected Percentage/Grade.
(Optional) Enter Cumulative Data: Add your previous GPA and Credits to see your new overall Cumulative GPA.Watch your results update in real-time in the blue panel!
