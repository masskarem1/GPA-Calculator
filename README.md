# GPA-Calculator
GPA &amp; Score Calculator designed specifically for our university's grading scale. It automatically converts percentages to grades, handles excluded/PE courses, saves your data locally so you never lose your progress, and even lets you compare your GPA with a friend! Just double-click the file to open it on your phone or laptop.

100% Accurate: Tailored to our exact grading scale (A*=4.0).

Smart Sync: Type your percentage, and it automatically selects the right grade!

Handles Everything: Easily uncheck courses with W, E, or PE grades.

Auto-Save: Closes the tab? No problem. Your grades are saved locally on your device.

Friendly Competition: Built-in hidden tab to compare your GPA and Score with a friend.

No App Needed: It's a single file that works instantly offline on any browser (Mobile or PC).
